Cat Breeds

Explore different cat breeds and learn more about their characteristics:

  • Persian Cat

    A Persian cat is known for its long fur and distinctive flat face.

  • Siamese Cat

    The Siamese cat is known for its striking blue almond-shaped eyes and short coat with color points.

  • Maine Coon

    The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds, known for its tufted ears and bushy tail.
